Now when I think about it, it feels really funny. We first met because of someone else's friendship, but today we have our own friendship. Out of all the unexpected things that happened during B.Tech.

The time we spent together recently has been so FUN. Whether we are laughing over silly things, just sharing updates, every moment is a memory.

I'm really happy that things happened this way. I'm glad that a person who was once just a random classmate became such a close friend.
